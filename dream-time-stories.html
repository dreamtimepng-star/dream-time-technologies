Dream Time Stories

Smart Cribs for Kids — calming bedtime stories for children aged 0–10 years. Designed to help children relax, imagine, learn values, and sleep peacefully.

Age Categories

  • 0–3 years: very short, soothing stories with repetition and comfort
  • 4–6 years: simple adventures, gentle lessons, friendly characters
  • 7–10 years: longer fantasy journeys with values and confidence-building

What We Deliver

  • Publishing-ready PDFs and eBooks
  • Narration scripts (audio/video-ready)
  • Illustration direction and scene prompts
  • Optional YouTube episode templates

Why It Matters

Good bedtime routines support learning, focus, and emotional wellbeing. Dream Time Stories helps families and schools encourage reading and calm sleep.

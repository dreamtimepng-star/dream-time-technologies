About Dream Time Technologies

Dream Time Technologies Limited is a Papua New Guinea based creative company producing children’s bedtime stories, narration scripts, and digital learning content. Our flagship program is Dream Time Stories (ages 0–10).

Mission

  • Support early literacy, creativity, and healthy sleep routines

Values

  • Safety: child-appropriate content standards
  • Impact: measurable delivery through KPIs and reporting
  • Integrity: transparency with donors and partners

  • Story packs tailored to age bands and partner needs
  • Budget tracking + risk management approach

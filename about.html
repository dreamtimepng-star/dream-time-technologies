About Dream Time Technologies Limited

Dream Time Technologies Limited is a Papua New Guinea–based creative and digital content company focused on children’s learning, wellbeing, and imagination.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality storytelling and learning content that helps children develop positive bedtime routines, stronger early literacy, and creative thinking.

What We Do

  • Dream Time Stories: bedtime fantasy stories for children aged 0–10
  • Publishing-ready PDFs and eBooks
  • Narration scripts for audio and video storytelling
  • Donor-ready programs with KPIs, reporting, and governance

Where We Work

Papua New Guinea — with a vision to scale regionally and globally through digital platforms.
